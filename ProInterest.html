Professional Interest

W3SCHOOLS

W3Schools is like an online school for web development. It offers easy-to-follow lessons, examples, and practice exercises for learning how to build websites. You can learn languages like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and there are also more advanced topics like PHP and SQL. It's a great place for beginners to start and for experienced developers to brush up on their skills. Plus, there's a helpful community forum where you can ask questions and get advice from other developers. 

Quillbot

QuillBot is a website that helps you rewrite or paraphrase text. You can paste in a sentence, paragraph, or even a whole article, and QuillBot will suggest alternative ways to express the same ideas. It's handy for rewording sentences to improve clarity or to avoid plagiarism. You can adjust the level of rewriting to make it more or less similar to the original text, and it's helpful for students, writers, and anyone who needs to rephrase text effectively.

Blackbox Chat AI

Blackbox Chat AI is a website where you can chat with an artificial intelligence (AI) program. It's like having a conversation with a computer. You can ask it questions, tell it things, or just chat for fun. The AI tries to understand what you're saying and responds in a way that makes sense. It's a cool way to see how AI technology works and to have interesting conversations without needing another person.

ChatGPT

ChatGPT is a platform where you can interact with an artificial intelligence! You can ask questions, have conversations, or seek assistance on various topics. It's a space where you can engage with AI in a conversational manner, just like chatting with a friend, but it's powered by technology. So, if you need information, advice, or just want to chat, ChatGPT is here for you!
